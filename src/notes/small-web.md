I'm getting really fed up with the corporate internet.  
It's killing the joy.

I miss the good old days when surfing the web actually meant *discovering* cool shit.  

A few people refused to let it die.  
Here are some places to find them.

## Discovery

- **[Kagi Small Web](https://kagi.com/smallweb)** - Recent indie posts
- **[Bear Blog](https://bearblog.dev/discover/)** - Indie blog discovery
- **[Neocities](https://neocities.org/browse)** - Personal site hosting
- **[Wiby](https://wiby.me/)** - Search engine for old-style sites
- **[Hotline Webring](https://hotlinewebring.club/)** - Personal site webring
- **[Webring](https://webring.xxiivv.com/)** - Webring
- **[250kb Club](https://250kb.club/)** - Sites under 250KB
- **[512KB Club](https://512kb.club/)** - Sites under 512KB
- **[1MB Club](https://1mb.club/)** - Sites under 1MB
